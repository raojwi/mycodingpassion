Warzone #1 "Verdansk, you will be missed... "
Verdansk is a fictional city in Kastovia, a fictional country that is featured in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare and serves as the main setting for the Special Ops operations and Ground War maps of Modern Warfare. Verdansk was also the original battleground for Call of Duty: Warzone. However, many fans online have found out that a number of the areas are based on various places in Donetsk, a city in Ukraine
The Caldera update "Although it was fun, it couldn't compete with Verdansk"
Caldera is a map available for Call of Duty: Warzone. The map was released on December 8th, 2021 with Season One of Call of Duty: Vanguard replacing Verdansk as the main map for Warzone. Caldera is a tropical map that is heavily inspired by real-life locations from around the world, and that played a part in World War II.
