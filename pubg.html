1ST WIN What is PUBG?
"PUBG stands for PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds1. It is a battle royale game developed by PUBG Studios and published by Krafton. In this game, up to one hundred players fight in a battle royale, a type of large-scale last man standing deathmatch where players fight to remain the last alive. Players can choose to enter the match solo, duo, or with a small team of up to four people. The last person or team alive wins the match."
